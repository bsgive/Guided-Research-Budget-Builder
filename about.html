About Guilded-Research-Budget-Builder

The reason for the tool’s creation, and what it accomplishes.

What this tool does:Preparing budgets for federally funded research projects involves a complex set of institutional and sponsor-specific regulations. Principal Investigators (PIs) must account for salaries, fringe benefits, tuition, travel, and indirect costs while ensuring compliance with both internal university policies and external funding agency guidelines. To reduce the administrative burden and risk of non-compliance, we propose the Guided Re- search Budget Builder —a menu-driven, multi-page application that guides PIs and co-PIs through the budgeting process. The tool removes the need for deep familiarity with institutional cost struc- tures and sponsor rules by encapsulating those within the application logic and backend database.

How it accomplishes this:The application provides a wizard-like, multi-step user interface. Budget development begins with a five-year high-level planning screen, allowing the PI to visually allocate personnel effort, student appointments, and other major cost categories. Based on this, the system automatically generates a fully detailed budget, which can be exported as an Excel spreadsheet.
